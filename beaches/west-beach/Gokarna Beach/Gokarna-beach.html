Gokarna beach

Gokarna Beach, located in Karnataka, is a serene coastal destination known for its unspoiled beauty and spiritual charm. Unlike the crowded beaches of Goa, Gokarna offers a calm atmosphere surrounded by temples, rocky shores, and golden sands. It is popular among backpackers and pilgrims alike, with nearby beaches like Om Beach and Kudle adding to its scenic appeal. Gokarna Beach is perfect for those seeking peace, yoga retreats, and a closer connection with nature.
