East India Waterfalls

East India's waterfalls are natural treasures, especially across the Northeast. Lush forests, towering drops and rich local legends define places like Meghalaya’s Nohkalikai and Nohsngithiang (Seven Sisters), Mizoram’s Vantawng, and Assam’s Akashiganga. These falls offer breathtaking views and a strong connection to the region’s vibrant ecosystem.
