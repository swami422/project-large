North India Temples

These North Indian temples—the Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib), Vaishno Devi, Kashi Vishwanath,Kedarnath, and Badrinath—are deeply significant pilgrimage sites. The Golden Temple in Amritsar is a Sikh gurdwara of immense beauty, while Vaishno Devi is a revered cave shrine in Jammu & Kashmir. Kashi Vishwanath in Varanasi is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas dedicated to Shiva, and the mountain temples of Kedarnath (Shiva) and Badrinath (Vishnu) in Uttarakhand are crucial components of the holy Char Dham Yatra.
