west India waterfalls

West India is blessed with scenic waterfalls that come alive during the monsoon season. From the mighty Dudhsagar Falls in Goa, cascading like streams of milk, to the serene Thoseghar Falls in Maharashtra and the refreshing Gira Falls in Gujarat, each waterfall offers breathtaking views and a peaceful escape into nature. Surrounded by lush forests and hills, these waterfalls are perfect destinations for travelers seeking beauty and tranquility.
